# Installing Lex Sovereign on Your Phone

Lex Sovereign is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**. Once deployed to Vercel, you can install it directly on your phone's home screen — it works like a native app.

---

## Step 1 — Deploy to Vercel (2 minutes)

1. Push this project to GitHub (or upload as ZIP at vercel.com/new)
2. Connect GitHub repo to Vercel
3. Add all environment variables from `.env.example`
4. Click Deploy
5. Your app is live at `https://your-app.vercel.app`

---

## Step 2 — Install on iPhone (iOS)

1. Open Safari on your iPhone
2. Go to `https://your-app.vercel.app/dashboard`
3. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow pointing up)
4. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Name it **"Lex Sovereign"**
6. Tap **Add**

The Lex Sovereign icon now appears on your home screen. Tap it to open — it launches full-screen with no browser bar, exactly like a native app.

**Works offline?** Partially — the UI loads, but AI queries require internet.

---

## Step 3 — Install on Android

1. Open Chrome on your Android phone
2. Go to `https://your-app.vercel.app/dashboard`
3. Tap the **3-dot menu** (top right)
4. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
5. Tap **Install**

The app appears in your app drawer and home screen.

---

## Mobile Tips

- The app is fully responsive — all features work on mobile
- The Approval Queue is especially useful on phone — approve or reject draft replies on the go
- The AI chat works great on mobile — type your question, get a streaming response
- Notifications: enable browser notifications for real-time approval alerts (future update)

## Custom Domain (Optional — Looks More Professional)

1. Buy a domain (e.g. `lexsovereign.app`) at Namecheap (~$12/year)
2. In Vercel dashboard: Settings → Domains → Add your domain
3. Follow Vercel's DNS instructions (takes 5 minutes)
4. Your app is now at `https://lexsovereign.app`

A custom domain makes it much easier to market and sell to clients.
